Oat & Chia Overnight

Ingredients:

    🥣 🌾 🌱 🔴 🫐 🥛 🍯 🌿

  • 2 tablespoons rolled oats
  • 2 tablespoons chia seeds
  • 3/4 cup milk (or any milk of choice) the quantity is depend how thick you want it to be
  • 2 tablespoon pomegranate seeds
  • handfull of blueberries
  • 1/2 teaspoon honey
  • A dash of cinnamon

    • *you may add more fruit if you like, usually i add 1 strawbery or quarter small mango too.

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Stir well to combine.
  3. Pour into a jar or container with a lid.
  4. Refrigerate overnight (or at least 6 hours).
  5. Serve cold and enjoy!
